GOVERNANCE, PRIVACY AND COMPLIANCE ANNEX – HYBRIDY TRUCK ECOSYSTEM

1. LEGAL NATURE AND UTILITY (UTILITY TOKEN)

The asset Brutos Coin (BRT) is classified strictly as an Infrastructure Utility Token. It serves as the unit of account and incentive within the decentralized physical infrastructure network (DePIN) of Hybridy Truck.

No Expectation of Passive Profit: The acquisition of BRT does not guarantee dividends, corporate participation, or fixed income. Its valuation is strictly linked to the adoption and utility of the logistics ecosystem.

2. PRIVACY AND TELEMETRY (LAW 81/2019 AND GDPR)

The operation of Hybridy Truck modules involves the collection of telemetry data (fuel consumption, geolocation, engine performance).

Pseudonymization: All fleet data is technically processed so that the identity of the driver or owner is not directly identifiable on the public network (Blockchain), in compliance with Law 81/2019 of Panama.

Purpose: Data is used exclusively for the optimization of the DePIN network and validation of the Proof of Efficiency.

3. REAL WORLD ASSETS (RWA) AND RISKS

The Hybridy Truck ecosystem integrates physical hardware with digital assets.

Operational Risk: The user acknowledges that failures in mechanical components or variations in diesel prices are inherent risks in the transport sector and may indirectly influence the demand for the ecosystem.

Secondary Market: Verum Wallet Freeport does not maintain, control, or guarantee liquidity on third-party exchanges. Price fluctuation is determined exclusively by market supply and demand.
